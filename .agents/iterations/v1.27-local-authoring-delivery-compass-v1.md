## 0. Position

V1.26 made narrative and World KB **reads** durable and productized local `assemble-moment`, but **User knowledge remained in-memory** and there is **no public write path** for worlds, timeline events, or World KB blocks. V1.27 closes the **local authoring loop** under a frozen architectural rule:

> **CLI-first for all surfaces that may later connect to platform; daemon focuses on pure local runtime (reads + orchestration + agent-host), not cloud proxies.**

When this compass conflicts with an active locked plan, the active plan wins for delivery batching.

## 2. Architecture (CLI-first)

```text
nexus42 creator / platform context
        │
        ▼
resolve_state_db_path()  ──►  ~/.nexus42/.../workspaces/<slug>/state.db
        │
        ├── narrative_write (new)     ── narrative_worlds, narrative_timeline_events
        ├── kb_store (SqliteKbStore)  ── kb_key_blocks
        ├── knowledge_store (new)     ── knowledge_entries
        └── reference_source (existing)

nexus-daemon-runtime
        │
        └── GET /v1/local/narrative/*  (read SqliteNarrativeGateway; no new POST)
```

**Invariant**: Do not add daemon POST routes for narrative, World KB, or User knowledge in V1.27. Theme H may add **validation-only** behavior on existing work-KB routes.
