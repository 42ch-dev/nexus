### AC4 — `JoinHandle::abort()` risk replaced by graceful shutdown signaling

**PASS**

- `CancellationToken` integrated into `ActionContext` for cooperative cancellation.
- `exit_starting()` in `crates/nexus42d/src/lifecycle/actions.rs` (lines 205-233):
  1. Calls `ctx.start_cancel.cancel()` to signal all in-flight tasks.
  2. Drains handles; skips already-finished tasks.
  3. Calls `handle.abort()` as fallback for non-finished tasks.
- Doc comment corrected (QC3 W-4 fixed): no longer claims a "grace window" — accurately describes immediate-abort after cancellation signal.
- `tokio::select!` races subsystem start against cancellation in entry actions.
