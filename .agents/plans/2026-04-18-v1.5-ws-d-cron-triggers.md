## Crate Selection Decision

**Decision: Hand-rolled implementation (no third-party scheduler crate)**

**Rationale**:
1. `tokio-cron-scheduler` requires wrappers for per-key serialization and graceful shutdown
2. DST risk mitigation requires external recompute logic тАФ same complexity as hand-rolled
3. Our use case is simpler: poll `scheduled_at <= now()` periodically, not full cron expressions
4. **Zero new crate dependencies** тАФ no justification needed per ┬з1.4
5. Already have `chrono` + `tokio::time` in workspace
6. Full control over all four hard constraints
