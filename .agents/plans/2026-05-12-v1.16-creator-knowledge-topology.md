## Goal

Extend `creator` with soul/memory/kb/logout subcommands, migrate `init`/`clone` → `creator workspace init`/`creator workspace clone`, migrate `world` → `sync world`, migrate `schedule` → `daemon schedule`, and hide remaining legacy top-level commands. After this plan, only 6 V2 groups are visible.

## Scope

### In scope

1. **Extend `creator` subcommands**:
   - `creator soul` — migrated from `soul` (thin delegation)
   - `creator memory` — migrated from `memory` (thin delegation)
   - `creator kb` — new stub for future KB multi-scope
   - `creator logout` — new (clear creator credentials/session)
   - Extend `creator workspace` with `init` (from `init`), `clone` (from `clone`), `link`/`unlink`/`status` (stubs)

2. **Extend `sync` subcommands**:
   - `sync world` — migrated from `world` (thin delegation)
   - `sync retry` — new stub

3. **Migrate `schedule` → `daemon schedule`** (thin delegation)

4. **Hide remaining legacy top-levels**:
   - `soul`, `memory`, `init`, `clone`, `world`, `schedule`

5. **Un-ignore `v2_target_creator_subcommands`**

6. **Un-ignore `v2_only_six_visible_command_groups`** (final — only 6 visible after this)

7. **Update `current_state_visible_command_groups`** (12 → 6 visible)

### Out of scope

- `creator kb` full implementation (Plan 5)
- `sync retry` full implementation
- Removing hidden legacy commands (later cleanup)
- `v2_target_sync_subcommands` (sync already has pull/push/status/resolve; retry may be stub)
