**T2 Design Decision**: `subscribe()` was moved off the `NexusAcpClient` trait onto `AcpSdkAdapter` as a direct method. Rationale: (1) `StreamReceiver` from `async-broadcast` is tightly coupled to SDK's broadcast implementation, (2) no consumer uses `subscribe()` through the trait abstraction, (3) creating `NexusStreamEvent`/`NexusEventStream` wrappers would add complexity for an unused feature.
