## 1. Problem Statement

Composite Work onboarding lived under `creator run start`. Atomic ops (`continue`, `resume`, `reconcile-chapters`) lived under `creator run` but are not presets. V1.45 splits into **`creator bootstrap`** (composite) and **`creator works`** (atomic only).

---

## 2. Goals

1. Add top-level **`nexus42 creator bootstrap`** with 1:1 flags from `run start`.
2. Add **`creator works inspire`** ← `run continue` (drop unimplemented `--preset`).
3. Add **`creator works reopen`** + **`creator works resume-chain`** ← `run resume`.
4. Add **`creator works reconcile-chapters`** ← `run reconcile-chapters`.
5. Optional positional `[<work_id>]` on migrated works commands.
6. Update `CreatorCommand` help tiers in `creator/mod.rs`.

---

## 3. Non-goals

- `creator works create` (Grill #11).
- Generic preset runner (P0).

---

## 4. Acceptance Criteria

1. `creator bootstrap --idea "…"` creates Work + schedules intake.
2. `creator works inspire --note "…"` POSTs inspiration only.
3. No `start`/`continue`/`resume`/`reconcile-chapters` under `creator run`.
4. `works` subcommands remain strictly single-purpose.
