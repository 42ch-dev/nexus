## 1. Problem Statement

V1.36 intentionally stopped at a single polished chapter. `novel-project-init` can seed multiple `work_chapters` rows, but the production preset and completion semantics are still effectively chapter-1 oriented. Ordinary serial novel writing requires Nexus to select the next not-started chapter, produce chapter 2..N, track progress, and only complete the Work when all planned chapters are finalized.

---

## 2. Goals

1. Convert V1.36 single-chapter semantics into a multi-chapter roadmap that can be implemented after P0 stabilizes input/gates.
2. Plan DF-62 explicitly: volume-aware frontmatter, volume outline semantics, and eventual `(work_id, volume, chapter)` key migration.
3. Define chapter selection rules: next `not_started` row, resume existing `draft`, and behavior when rows and files disagree.
4. Define `creator run status` progress UX for multi-chapter Works.
5. Decide which parts are V1.37 implementation vs deferred roadmap.

---
