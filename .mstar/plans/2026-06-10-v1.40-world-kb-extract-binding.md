## 1. Problem Statement

The FL-E `persist` stage maps to `creator kb queue-extract` + `kb-extract`, but novel Works do not bind extraction target to `work.world_id`. Chapter facts do not flow back into World KB with SourceAnchors.

---

## 2. Goals

1. Auto-chain / persist stage enqueues extract with `work.world_id` when World-bound.
2. `kb-extract` reads finalized chapter body + outline indexes; creates/updates taxonomy items.
3. SourceAnchors link KB items to chapter path and timeline event when available.
4. Worldless Works: skip World promotion (no silent World creation).
5. Explicit promotion from event-index / foreshadowing when user/agent marks promote.

---

## 3. Non-goals

- Platform publish (DF-59).
- Automatic promotion of every foreshadowing row without explicit mark.
- Multi-volume chronology.

---

## 4. Acceptance Criteria

1. World-bound Work after chapter finalize: extract job targets `work.world_id`.
2. At least one hermetic test proves KeyBlock created with SourceAnchor to chapter path.
3. Worldless Work: no World KeyBlocks created by persist/extract.
4. Invalid/missing `world_id` at extract time fails with remediation (not silent skip for World-bound).
5. DF-63 row closable in deferred tracker after P3 ships.
