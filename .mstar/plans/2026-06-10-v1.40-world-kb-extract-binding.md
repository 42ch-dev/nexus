## 1. Problem Statement

The FL-E `persist` stage maps to `creator kb queue-extract` + `kb-extract`, but novel Works do not bind extraction target to `work.world_id`. Chapter facts do not flow back into World KB with SourceAnchors. Legacy extract uses work-entry semantics and wrong taxonomy. Ingest domain logic lives in orchestration capability instead of `nexus-kb`.

---

## 2. Goals

1. **Generic artifact ingest job** on `kb_extract_jobs`: `source_kind`, `source_locator`, `profile_hint` (see architecture doc ┬¦5). V1.40 ships `work_chapter` + `profile_hint=novel` only.
2. `nexus-kb::ingest_from_artifact(job)` ŌĆö taxonomy validation, KeyBlock upsert, SourceAnchors (domain SSOT).
3. Refactor `kb-extract` preset: LLM inner_graph only; capability `kb.ingest_artifact` thin-delegates to `nexus-kb`.
4. Auto-chain / persist enqueues ingest job with `work.world_id` when World-bound.
5. Worldless Works: skip World promotion (no silent World creation).
6. **Tail slice**: `novel-review-master` optional `sync_world_kb` state ŌĆö child schedule `kb-extract`, `exit_when: schedule_terminal`, gated by `preset.input.refresh_kb` (default true for World-bound).

## 4. Acceptance Criteria

1. World-bound Work after chapter finalize: job uses artifact locator `{ relative_path: Stories/ch<nn>-<slug>.md }` and `work.world_id`.
2. Hermetic test: ingest ŌåÆ KeyBlock with SourceAnchor to chapter path; taxonomy from P1.
3. Worldless Work: no World KeyBlocks created by persist/ingest.
4. Invalid/missing `world_id` at ingest time fails with remediation for World-bound Works.
5. `creator kb queue-extract` accepts `--source-kind work_chapter` (and `--chapter N` sugar for novel).
6. Review-master with `refresh_kb=true` waits for child kb-extract before findings review (hermetic or integration test).
7. DF-63 closable in deferred tracker after P3 ships.
