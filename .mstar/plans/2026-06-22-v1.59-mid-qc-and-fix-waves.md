## Goal

Track the single-wave QC tri-review + mid-QA + fix-wave rhythm across both implement plans (P0, P1). Ensure each plan's QC reports are filed, fix-waves are executed, and re-reviews are completed before P-last begins. This plan is a PM-side checklist — no product code, no spec changes.

---

## Scope

- **T1**: Wave 1 evidence gate. After P0 and P1 complete implementation and merge to `iteration/v1.59`: collect 6 QC reports (qc1/qc2/qc3 for each plan), execute mid-QA, track fix-waves, collect re-review reports. Wave 1 advances only when both P0 and P1 are accepted (all 6 QC Approve or PM-override with documented rationale).
- **T2**: Post-wave rhythm summary. After Wave 1 completes: compile total QC report count (6), fix-wave count, mid-QA verdicts (2), residual additions. Verify no implement plan entered review with unresolved cross-plan dependencies.
- **T3**: P-last readiness gate. Verify both P0 and P1 are merged to `iteration/v1.59`, all QC reports are Approve, all mid-QA verdicts are Pass, and all fix-waves are resolved. Signal P-last dispatch readiness to PM.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Wave 1: 6 QC reports filed (3 per plan). Both P0 and P1 accepted (all Approve or PM-override). Mid-QA executed for both plans (2 mid-QA reports). Fix-waves tracked.
- [ ] Total: 6 QC reports + 2 mid-QA reports.
- [ ] P-last dispatched only after both P0 and P1 are accepted on `iteration/v1.59`.
- [ ] Cross-plan dependency verification: no P0/P1 merge conflicts unresolved; `nexus-local-db` shared changes reconciled.
