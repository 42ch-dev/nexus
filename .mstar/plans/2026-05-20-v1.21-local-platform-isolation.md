## Objective

Implement the first full **local/cloud hard isolation** and crate split per the long-term spec:

1. Remove platform sync and creator registration from daemon Local API.
2. Rename/split crates (`nexus-cloud-sync`, `nexus-cloud-domain`, `nexus-creator`, `nexus-creator-memory`, `nexus-kb`, `nexus-knowledge`, `nexus-narrative`, `nexus-moment-context-assembly`).
3. Enforce **contracts-first** in `nexus-creator` and `nexus-cloud-domain` (no duplicate platform DTOs).
4. Wire CLI register/verify/sync through **cloud line** only; stub orchestration `sync.*` on daemon builds.
