## Goal

Convert 9 DF-47 catalog-only capability IDs to fully shipped host tools. P1 (Track B) does NOT touch this function, but if any concurrent edit lands (e.g. a hotfix), merge conflict may occur. Mitigation: P0 is the sole track adding host_tool entries in V1.59.
