## 1. Problem Statement

`creator bootstrap` only schedules `creative-brief-intake` for **new** Works. Existing Works have no first-class intake re-trigger. `creator works reconcile-chapters` mutates filesystem/DB without `--dry-run` or confirmation (unlike `works rules reset`).

3. Close R-V147P1-01 and R-V148P4-W2 in `status.json`.
