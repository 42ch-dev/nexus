## Goal

Track the 3-wave QC tri-review + mid-QA + fix-wave rhythm across all 4 implement plans (P0, P1, P2, P3). Ensure each wave's QC reports are filed, fix-waves are executed, and re-reviews are completed before the next wave dispatches. This plan is a PM-side checklist — no product code, no spec changes.

---

## Scope

- **T1**: Wave 1 evidence gate. After P0 and P1 complete implementation and merge to `iteration/v1.58`: collect 6 QC reports (qc1/qc2/qc3 for each plan), execute mid-QA, track fix-waves, collect re-review reports. Wave 1 advances only when both P0 and P1 are accepted (all 6 QC Approve or PM-override with documented rationale).
- **T2**: Wave 2 evidence gate. After P2 merges to `iteration/v1.58`: collect 3 QC reports, execute mid-QA, track fix-wave, collect re-review reports (if any). Wave 2 advances when P2 is accepted.
- **T3**: Wave 3 evidence gate. After P3 merges to `iteration/v1.58`: collect 3 QC reports, execute mid-QA, track fix-wave, collect re-review reports (if any). Wave 3 advances when P3 is accepted.
- **T4**: Wave rhythm summary. After all 3 waves complete: compile total QC report count, fix-wave count, mid-QA verdicts, residual additions. Verify no implement plan entered review with unresolved cross-plan dependencies.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Wave 1: 6 QC reports filed (3 per plan). Both P0 and P1 accepted (all Approve or PM-override). Mid-QA executed. Fix-waves tracked.
- [ ] Wave 2: 3 QC reports filed. P2 accepted. Mid-QA executed. Fix-waves tracked.
- [ ] Wave 3: 3 QC reports filed. P3 accepted. Mid-QA executed. Fix-waves tracked.
- [ ] Total: 12 QC reports + up to 4 mid-QA reports across 3 waves.
- [ ] No wave dispatched before prior wave's plans are all accepted.
- [ ] Cross-plan dependency verification: P2 dispatched only after P0+P1 accepted; P3 dispatched only after P0+P1+P2 accepted.

## Risks

- **QC backlog**: If any implement plan generates a high volume of QC findings, re-review cycles may delay subsequent waves. Mitigation: PM monitors residual count per wave; if >3 medium+ findings per plan, consider extending the fix-wave window.
- **Cross-plan merge conflicts**: P2 and P3 depend on prior plans merged to integration. If a fix-wave on P0 changes a file that P2 also touches, merge conflicts occur. Mitigation: fix-waves are surgical (not refactors); PM resolves conflicts on `iteration/v1.58`.
