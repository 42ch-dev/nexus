## 1. Problem Statement

P0 auto-chains stages but research must produce durable KB/reference artifacts that produce and review consume — as a **side effect** that does not fork the chain.

---

## 2. Goals

1. Ensure `research` preset runs correctly inside auto-chain (gates, `fl_e_stage`, Work seed).
2. Research outputs land in reference_sources / Work KB paths per existing contracts.
3. Produce and review presets read refreshed KB context on next state (no second driver schedule).
4. Status shows "KB updated; next produce will include research" after research completes.

---

## 3. Non-goals

- New World KB taxonomy (DF-63).
- Findings or review-master presets.
- Platform cloud assemble.

---

## 4. Acceptance Criteria

1. Auto-chain from intake includes research before first produce without manual advance.
2. After research completes, at least one reference/KB artifact is queryable for the Work.
3. Subsequent `novel-writing` prompt context includes research-derived material (via existing assembly paths).
4. Research completion does not cancel or duplicate the auto-chain driver.
