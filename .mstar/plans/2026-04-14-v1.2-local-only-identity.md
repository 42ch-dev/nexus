## Scope

Implement dual local identity strategy for `local_only` mode:
1. **Anonymous/temporary identity** — ephemeral, no persistent store required
2. **Local persistent identity** — stored locally, no platform registration dependency

This is the foundation for WS2b (runtime) and directly satisfies regression requirements R1 and R2.
