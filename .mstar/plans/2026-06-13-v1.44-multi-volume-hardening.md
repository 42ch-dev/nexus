## 1. Problem Statement

V1.42 P1 shipped multi-volume PK and volume-aware auto-chain, but QC flagged fragile completion semantics (`is_work_completed` flat chapter comparison) and supervisor `NextChapter` ignoring `next_volume`, causing volume context loss before schedule enqueue.

---

## 2. Goals

1. Fix `is_work_completed` to be volume-aware per novel-workflow-profile invariants.
2. Propagate `next_volume` through supervisor `NextChapter` match arm into schedule `preset.input`.
3. Regression tests for cross-volume chapter advance + completion stop.
4. Disposition R-V142P1-F-002 and F-004 (fix or documented waive with test).

---

## 3. Non-goals

- New volume PK schema (V1.42 migration sufficient)
- Cross-volume auto-chain **new** product features beyond hardening
- DF-69 / review-master CLI

---

## 4. Acceptance Criteria

1. Hermetic test: Work with 2 volumes completes only when all volumes satisfy completion predicate.
2. Hermetic test: supervisor enqueue includes correct `volume` in chapter context after volume boundary.
3. `creator works status` shows volume column consistently across volumes.
4. Residuals closed in status.json.
