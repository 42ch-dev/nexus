## 1. Problem Statement

V1.42 P1 shipped multi-volume PK, `next_chapter_volume_aware`, and `evaluate_after_persist_volume_aware`. QC flagged two remaining edges:

1. **F-002**: `is_work_completed` in `nexus-local-db` uses flat `current_chapter >= total_planned_chapters` and `list_chapters().len() == total` ÔÇö fragile when volume-aware chapter numbering repeats across volumes.
2. **F-004**: `supervisor.rs` `ChainAction::NextChapter` logs `next_volume` but calls `enqueue_auto_chain_step(..., Some(next_chapter), ...)` without volume; `WorkFields` / `build_auto_chain_schedule` have no `volume` field, so `novel-writing` preset input loses cross-volume context.

**Out of scope**: R-V142P1-QC1-F-003 (reconcile volume-aware) ÔÇö **resolved** V1.43 P-last.

---

## 2. Goals

1. Harden `work_chapters::is_work_completed` for multi-volume Works per ┬ž6.1 invariants (all rows finalized across volumes; correct progress predicate).

3. Extend / fix `supervisor_cross_volume.rs` and add `work_chapters` completion regression tests.
4. Close F-002 and F-004 in `status.json` with `target: V1.44 P2`.
