## 1. Problem Statement

DF-65 and DF-66 are roadmap-only. V1.39 implements rules files and Logs/ structure with write discipline and prompt readers.

---

## 2. Goals

1. Layer 1: `embedded-rules/writing-craft.md` + optional `~/.nexus42/rules/writing-craft.md` override.
2. Layer 2: `Works/<work_ref>/Rules/novel-rules.md` scaffold on novel init.
3. Layer 3: append-only `novel-rules-history.md` on rule changes.
4. Logs subdirs: `brainstorm/`, `write/`, `review/`, reserved `publish/`; excluded from chapter sync.
5. Presets read rules layers where applicable; reflection-loop may append rule suggestions (P1 integration).

---

## 3. Non-goals

- World KB content in rules files.
- Platform publish logs content.

---

## 4. Acceptance Criteria

1. Novel init creates `Rules/` stubs and empty Logs subdirs per spec.
2. `novel-writing` reads Layer 1 + Layer 2 when present.
3. Rule update appends history row with timestamp + reason.
4. Chapter sync scan still limited to `Stories/` only.
