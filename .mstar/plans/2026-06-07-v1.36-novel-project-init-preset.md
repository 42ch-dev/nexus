## 1. Problem Statement

Starting a novel Work today jumps from brief intake into `novel-writing` without a dedicated **project bootstrap** phase: `work_ref`, planned chapter count, directory scaffold, and high-level creative direction are implicit or demo-grade. Reference systems use a grill-me init flow; Nexus needs an orchestration preset, not a CLI wizard.

---

## 2. Goals

1. Add embedded preset `novel-project-init` with `run_intents: [work_init]`.
2. ACP multi-turn grill-me collects: working title, genre/tone hooks, `total_planned_chapters`, `work_ref` slug, optional worldbinding seeds.
3. On success: create `Works/<work_ref>/` scaffold (README, work-status, Outlines/, Stories/, optional Worldbuilding/).
4. Persist `work_profile: novel`, `work_ref`, `total_planned_chapters` on Work via Local API.
5. CLI: `creator run start` may offer `--init-preset novel-project-init` or auto-suggest when scaffold missing.

---

## 3. Non-goals

- Auto-chain into `novel-writing` (user explicitly starts produce after init)
- Platform publish IDs
- Automatic switch to next novel after completion (DF-59)
