## 1. Problem Statement

Authors cannot audit or extract from an existing chapter body on demand without entering the FL-E auto-chain. DF-69 was deferred at V1.43 grill-me as the natural follow-up after author visibility shipped.

---

## 2. Goals

1. Implement embedded preset `novel-manuscript-audit` (or preset pair) with `mode=review|extract`.
2. Wire CLI entry: `creator run audit-chapter` (lock exact flags in T1 before code).
3. Review mode: report under `Logs/review/` + optional findings upsert.
4. Extract mode: World-bound `kb.extract_work` without queue ceremony.
5. Hermetic tests for both modes + worldless extract fail-closed.
