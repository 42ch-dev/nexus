## 1. Problem Statement

Open findings older than 96h need local-first escalation per V1.37 P3 roadmap — daemon scheduled task + status banner, opt-in auto-action only.

---

## 2. Goals

1. Daemon lifecycle task (24h interval) queries `findings` where `status=open` and `created_at < now-96h`.
2. Structured log + optional notification surface.
3. `creator run status` banner with count and `review-master` hint.
4. Work-level or CLI opt-in for automatic `review-master` scheduling after banner (default: off).

---

## 3. Non-goals

- Redis, external cron, platform workers.
- Automatic master action without opt-in.

---

## 4. Acceptance Criteria

1. Seeded finding older than 96h appears in status banner.
2. Daemon task runs without blocking main loop; errors logged.
3. Default: no auto schedule of review-master; opt-in documented and tested.

---

- [ ] T2: Query + log open stale findings.
- [ ] T3: Status banner formatting.
