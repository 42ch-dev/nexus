## 1. Problem Statement

V1.37 shipped foundation-first novel-writing reliability and recorded DF-62 multi-chapter semantics as a roadmap. V1.38 now needs a docs-only harness prepare wave that opens the implementation-ready iteration without touching product code: one compass, one prepare plan, and two executable future implementation plans for serial writing.

V1.38 must keep those boundaries explicit so P0/P1 can be implemented and verified without accidentally claiming the broader novel platform.
