## 1. Problem Statement

V1.36 made a strong architectural choice: novel worldbuilding is cross-Work and belongs in World KB, not in `Works/<work_ref>/Worldbuilding/`. It shipped the `world_id` binding and advisory `world_refs`, but not the full user experience for creating a World, validating references, injecting World KB context, or promoting chapter facts back into the World. V1.37 needs a roadmap that keeps the choice coherent without blocking the P0/P1 foundation work.

---

## 2. Goals

1. Plan the DF-63 slice: World KB taxonomy, Work binding validation, `world_refs` enforcement, and Work -> World KB extraction.
2. Decide whether V1.37 implements any World KB behavior or only locks the spec and roadmap.
3. Define ordinary-user UX for existing World, new World, and worldless Work paths.
4. Keep the rejected per-Work `Worldbuilding/` subtree out of normative docs.
5. Identify dependencies on P0 input wiring and P1 multi-chapter context.

---

## 3. Non-goals

- Platform or social World fork features.
- Platform publish (DF-59).
- Automatic multi-novel switch (DF-60).

## 4.1 Audit results (T1)

Current specs already define the **scope** and storage substrate for World KB, but not the novel-specific narrative taxonomy. `entity-scope-model.md` §1–§5 says World owns narrative KB graph assets, KeyBlocks, and SourceAnchors through `nexus-kb` + `nexus-narrative`; `local-db-schema.md` §4.1.2 provides a generic persisted shape (`kb_key_blocks.world_id`, `block_type`, `canonical_name`, `status`, `revision`, `body_json`, anchors, and active uniqueness by `(world_id, block_type, canonical_name)`). `novel-workflow-profile.md` §3.5/§5.2 already rejects per-Work `Worldbuilding/`, defines `world_id`, advisory `world_refs`, and prompt-time World context at a high level. What is missing for novel drafting is a canonical **narrative category vocabulary**, minimum `body_json` fields per category, explicit create-new-World UX, validation timing for `world_id`/`world_refs`, and the chapter→World KB extraction/promotion path. P2 fills those as roadmap/spec contracts without implementing new code.

## 4.2 Scope decision (T8)

V1.37 P2 is **roadmap-only** for product code. The deliverable is the taxonomy and contract language in `entity-scope-model.md` + `novel-workflow-profile.md`, plus DF-63 tracker disposition. Implementation is deferred to V1.37+ because the work decomposes into at least five plan-sized, non-surgical items: `creator world create`, a stricter `nexus-kb` category/schema layer, World KB query capability for prompts, `world_refs` validation at outline/finalize gates, and `kb-extract` binding to `work.world_id`. P0 already landed gate/input plumbing; P2 should not destabilize it with a broad implementation wave.
