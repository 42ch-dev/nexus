## 0. Position

V1.43 closed **discoverability**: authors have `docs/novel-writing-quickstart.md`, aligned CLI copy, and author-visible completion/findings surfaces. The largest remaining gap for **ordinary authors** is **actionable quality work on demand** — reviewing or extracting from an already-written chapter **without** entering the full FL-E auto-chain, then routing findings through a **discoverable** review-master surface.

V1.44 delivers that **on-demand audit closed loop** (DF-69), converges the deferred **review-master CLI** gap (R-V143P0-002), hardens **multi-volume serial** runtime edges shipped in V1.42, and selectively closes **author-desk** residuals from V1.38–V1.43.

When this compass conflicts with a later locked implementation plan, the active plan wins for delivery batching only. Normative behavior changes still require spec updates before implementation.
