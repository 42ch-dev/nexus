# Brainstorm: Cluster

Review all the brainstormed concepts for **{{preset.input.topic}}** and
group them into 4-6 thematic clusters.

For each cluster:
- **Cluster name**: a short evocative title
- **Concepts**: which concept numbers belong here
- **Core tension**: what conflict or question unifies these ideas
- **Potential**: a one-line assessment of narrative promise

Prioritize clusters that contain both safe and risky concepts — those often
yield the most original work.
